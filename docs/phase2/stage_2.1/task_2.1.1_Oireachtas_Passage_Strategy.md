# Task 2.1.1: Oireachtas Passage Strategy & Enactment Protocol

## Objective
Secure enactment of the Criminal Justice Oversight Commission Bill with minimal substantive amendment during Dáil/Seanad stages, ensuring Presidential signature within target timeline.

## Stage-by-Stage Management Plan

### Dáil Éireann

| Stage | Key Actions | Risk Mitigation | Timeline Target |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **First Stage** | Present Bill; circulate Explanatory & Financial Memoranda | Ensure all PLS/consultation responses referenced in Second Stage speech | Day 0 |
| **Second Stage** | Minister’s opening speech emphasising cross-party consensus; address residual concerns from PLS report | Pre-brief opposition spokespersons; prepare amendment negotiation matrix | Week 2–3 |
| **Committee Stage** | Section-by-section scrutiny; table government amendments addressing accepted PLS recommendations | Resist non-government amendments expanding jurisdiction beyond constitutional limits; offer regulatory alternatives where possible | Week 4–6 |
| **Report Stage** | Final amendments; confirm no new issues introduced at Committee Stage | Whip coordination; verify financial implications of any late amendments | Week 7 |
| **Fifth Stage** | Formal passing; no debate unless exceptional circumstances | Confirm pairing arrangements for absent TDs | Week 8 |

### Seanad Éireann

| Stage | Key Actions | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **All Stages** | Leverage Seanad’s technical/review function; anticipate legal academic senators’ focus on constitutional boundaries | Prepare detailed briefing on Head 19 exclusions; engage Independent senators early |
| **Amendments** | Accept only technical/drafting amendments; refer substantive changes back to Dáil if necessary | Maintain message consistency between Houses |

### Presidential Signature & Promulgation

| Action | Responsible | Deadline |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Final vellum preparation | OPC / Clerk of Dáil | Within 2 days of Seanad passage |
| Submission to President | Taoiseach’s Department | Within 5 days of final passage |
| Article 26 referral assessment | AGO (pre-emptive) | Completed before submission |
| Signing & promulgation | President / Iris Oifigiúil | Within 7 days of receipt |
| Commencement Order drafting | DoJ Legislation Unit | Parallel track; ready upon signature |

## Amendment Negotiation Matrix

| Amendment Type | Government Position | Negotiation Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Technical/drafting improvements | Accept | Acknowledge contributor; incorporate immediately |
| Strengthened safeguards (data, whistleblowers) | Accept in principle | Offer enhanced regulatory specification rather than primary law amendment |
| Expanded jurisdiction (judiciary/DPP) | Reject firmly | Cite AGO advice + constitutional risk; offer MoU or reporting mechanism instead |
| Reduced powers/independence | Reject | Cite EU/GRECO non-compliance risk; reference international comparators |
| Resourcing/funding guarantees | Refer to Estimates process | Commit to separate Vote in next budget cycle; note in ministerial speech |

## Contingency Protocols

| Scenario | Trigger | Response |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Article 26 Referral | President refers Bill to Supreme Court | AGO leads defence; DoJ prepares public communication explaining constitutional validity; pause commencement preparations |
| Money Message Required | Ceann Comhairle determines Bill involves public expenditure | Fast-track money message through Committee on Budgetary Oversight; coordinate with DPENDR |
| Dáil Dissolution During Passage | Election called before Fifth Stage | Bill lapses; preserve all committee stage amendments for reintroduction in next Dáil; maintain shadow board recruitment in abeyance |
| Substantive Opposition Amendment Passes | Government loses vote on jurisdictional expansion | Immediate AGO consultation; consider recommittal or accept with qualifying amendment protecting constitutional boundary |

## Post-Enactment Immediate Actions
1.  Publish enacted Act on electronic Irish Statute Book (eISB) within 48 hours.
2.  Issue press release confirming enactment and next steps (Shadow Board appointment).
3.  Notify EU Commission and GRECO secretariat of transposition milestone.
4.  Activate commencement order for establishment day provisions.
