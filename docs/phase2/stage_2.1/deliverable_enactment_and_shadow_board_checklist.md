## ✅ LEGISLATIVE ENACTMENT

- [ ] Bill passed all Dáil stages without fatal amendment
- [ ] Bill passed all Seanad stages (or Dáil considered Seanad amendments)
- [ ] Final text verified against intended policy outcomes
- [ ] Vellum prepared and signed by Ceann Comhairde/Cathaoirleach
- [ ] Bill presented to President and signed into law
- [ ] Act published in Iris Oifigiúil and eISB
- [ ] Commencement order signed for establishment day provisions
- [ ] EU/GRECO notification of enactment dispatched

- [ ] Cross-party Oireachtas Selection Panel constituted

- [ ] Presidential appointment letters issued

- [ ] Media announcement coordinated with DoJ Communications

- [ ] All three documents recommended by Shadow Board for adoption
- [ ] DoJ legal compliance check completed (policy non-interference confirmed)
- [ ] Templates for annual declarations and ad-hoc disclosures prepared
- [ ] Independent ethics officer identification process initiated

- [ ] Interim meeting venue secured (DoJ premises or neutral location)

- [ ] Minute-taking template aligned with draft Standing Orders

- [ ] No Shadow Board member has current political affiliation

- [ ] Selection panel minutes demonstrate merit-based, non-partisan process
- [ ] Minister’s acceptance letter references panel recommendation explicitly
- [ ] Probity advisor report confirms process integrity
- [ ] All declarations cross-checked against Register of Interests, Lobbying Register, and Companies Register
