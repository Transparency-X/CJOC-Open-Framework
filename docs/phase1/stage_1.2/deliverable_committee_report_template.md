## DEPARTMENTAL RESPONSE PROTOCOL

Upon receipt of the Committee Report, the Department shall:

1.  Acknowledge receipt within **3 working days**.
2.  Prepare detailed response to each recommendation within **15 working days**, categorised as:
    -   **Accepted:** Will be incorporated into revised Heads/Bill
    -   **Accepted in Principle:** Will be addressed through alternative means (regulation, MoU, guidance)
    -   **Not Accepted:** With detailed rationale (constitutional, financial, or policy grounds)
    -   **Under Further Review:** Requires additional consultation or legal advice
3.  Submit revised General Scheme to Government incorporating accepted recommendations within **25 working days**.
4.  Publish Departmental response on gov.ie concurrently with revised Scheme submission.
